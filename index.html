Why Choose us?

With over 40 years of accumulated experience in the aluminum industry, our team has gained deep knowledge and advanced techniques in this field. We also possess more than 4 years of specialized expertise in the manufacturing of high-quality exterior aluminum cladding doors.

Over the years, our operations have expanded and our expertise in door manufacturing and architectural facades has grown significantly. We have proudly contributed to prominent projects across Saudi Arabia and the Gulf region, playing a key role in urban development. With our substantial capabilities, we are actively implementing strategic expansion plans to strengthen the leadership of "Business Pillars" in the aluminum industry—where our work is now considered a benchmark for quality and excellence in the Saudi market.
